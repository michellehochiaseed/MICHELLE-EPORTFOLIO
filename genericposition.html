Leadership & Contributions 🌟

Hi there! 👋👋👋 My leadership journey began in middle school, where I took on various roles to sharpen my skills. I proudly served as the Social Science Club President, the Head of School Librarian, and led as the First Aid Group Leader in the Malaysian Red Crescent Society. These experiences shaped my ability to manage responsibilities while building meaningful connections with my peers. 🏫✨

During my matriculation days, I continued to hone my organizational skills as the Treasurer for the Chinese Cultural Club, the Rubik's Cube Club, and the Kelab Rakan Perpustakaan (KERAP). 💰 These positions taught me the importance of financial management, teamwork, and cultural preservation. It was truly rewarding to contribute to both academic and extracurricular communities during this time. 📚🧩

Currently, I am an active member of the Bureau of Cultural Exhibition for the Pesta Tanglung UTM, where I work on bringing cultural events to life. 🏮🌟 I am always eager to explore new opportunities to grow and make a positive impact, and my journey is far from over. Stay tuned for more updates! 🚀🔥🔥
