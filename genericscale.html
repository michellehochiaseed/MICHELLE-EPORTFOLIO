🥇STEM INNOVATION COMPETITION🥇

During my matriculation era, I took part in a long-term innovation competition, from Campus to National Level, alongside my talented teammates: Xin Ying, Fung Maye, and Katricia. 💻🪄👑 Here’s a look at our full journey: Scroll down to see how we made it through each stage of this exciting challenge!

QuantifyPro Scale | Overview of our product - How to use it? 

📙Stage 1: In-campus Competition📙

July 2023👉🏻August 2023

During the in-campus competition, we faced off against 20 other teams for a chance to secure one of the two spots available for the national level. 🏆 We proudly earned 2nd place and managed to secure one of those spots! 🎉



📕Stage 2: Matriculation Science Carnival (MASCAR)📕

August 2023👉🏻October 2023

We upgraded and presented our innovation project, QuantifyPro Scale, at the STEM Creator Competition under the organization of MASCAR at Penang Matriculation College.🏫 During this few months, we spent countless nights working tirelessly, learning Arduino, programming, soldering, 3D model rendering and creating Looker Studio dashboard from scratch. The journey was exhausting, but it pushed us to learn and grow at an incredible pace, yielding valuable skills along the way. In the end, our hard work paid off✨ as we stood out among the many teams and earned the Gold Medal🥇👑



📗Stage 3: PIITRAM 2024📗

August 2023👉🏻February 2024

We were honored to be selected by our college to represent in the Pre-University Matriculation Innovation Competition (PIITRAM) 2024. This was an online innovation competition that required us to present through virtual presentations and recorded videos. 🎥 To prepare for this stage, we enhanced our original QuantifyPro Scale with multiple upgrades, including new functions, improved aesthetics, and advanced features for the app and Looker Studio dashboard. 🔧📊 It is worth mentioning that we successfully obtained the MyIPO Copyright for our innovation! Ultimately, our hard work and dedication earned us another Gold Medal in this competition, marking a remarkable conclusion to our journey! 🥇
